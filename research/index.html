Research

Research on the dynamics of the ocean circulation and its role in climate

The Experimental Oceanography group exploits novel observations and technologies to investigate the dynamics of the ocean circulation in a changing climate. The research problems that we tackle span from small-scale oceanic turbulence to the large-scale meridional overturning circulation, and our approaches use a range of observing technologies in order to span the spatial and temporal scales of oceanic proceses.

Current projects:

  • Explaining and Predicting the Ocean Conveyor (Horizon Europe). Link (external): EPOC
  • Targeted Experiment to Reconcile Increased Freshwater with Increased Convection (EU H2020 ERC Starting Grant fellowship). Link (external): TERIFIC overview
